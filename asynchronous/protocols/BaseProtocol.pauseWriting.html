    Function BaseProtocol.pauseWriting

    pauseWriting() and resumeWriting() calls are paired – pauseWriting() is called once when the buffer goes strictly over the high-water mark (even if subsequent writes increases the buffer size even more), and eventually resumeWriting() is called once when the buffer size reaches the low-water mark.

    Called when the transport’s buffer goes over the high-water mark.

    Prototype
